Lending

Find the Right Loan for Your Situation

Our trusted lending professionals will match you with the right loan program — whether you're buying your first home, refinancing, or investing. No pressure, just honest guidance.

FHA Loan

Low Down Payment

3.5% Down

Government-backed loan ideal for first-time buyers or those with lower credit scores. Flexible qualification requirements with competitive rates.

VA Loan

For Veterans & Active Military

0% Down

Zero down payment, no PMI, and competitive interest rates — exclusively for veterans, active-duty military, and eligible surviving spouses.

Conventional Loan

Standard Home Purchase

3–20% Down

The most common loan type. Best rates for borrowers with strong credit. Avoid PMI with 20% down. Available for primary, second homes, and investments.

Jumbo Loan

High-Value Properties

$800K+

For home purchases that exceed conventional loan limits. Competitive rates for well-qualified buyers purchasing higher-priced SoCal homes.

CalHFA

Down Payment Assistance

Up to $150K

California Housing Finance Agency programs help first-time buyers with down payment and closing cost assistance. Multiple programs available based on income and location.

USDA Loan

Rural & Suburban Areas

0% Down

Zero down payment for eligible rural and suburban properties. Some areas along the 210 corridor may qualify. Ask a lender about eligibility for your target area.
