We share your information only as follows:

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information below. We will respond within 45 days.
